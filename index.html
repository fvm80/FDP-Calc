FDP Calculator

Version: 1.0.0

FDP Calculator is a simple tool for planning crew duties in accordance with Flight Time Limitations (FTL). It is divided into two tabs: SIMPLE and SCHEDULE.
In the SIMPLE tab, FTL is calculated based on:
Start of duty - the reporting time for duty,
No. of planned sectors - the number of sectors planned for one duty,
Split Start - STA of flight preceding split,
Split End - STD of flight following split.
